How do I sign in to my GoodCrypto Server?

  1. Connect to the Goodcrypto Server website.
  2. Click the Sign in menu
  3. Enter the email address and password that you received via email from your GoodCrypto Server or if you're the administrator, then the email address and password you supplied when configuring your GoodCrypto Server.

Reminder: goodcrypto-private-server.sourceforge.io never receives a copy of passwords used by your GoodCrypto Server so keep them in a safe place.

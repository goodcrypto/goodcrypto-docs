How do I change options for GoodCrypto Mail?

You must have administrative privileges to change options.

  1. Connect to your Goodcrypto Server website.
  2. Sign in
  3. Click on Mail in the menu
  4. Click on Options button. This button only appears if your signed in as an administrator.
  5. Click on the MTA address

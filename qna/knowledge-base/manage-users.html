How do I manage users?

Your GoodCrypto private server automatically creates a user every time someone in your company sends or receives email. Each user receives a message with their credentials when their account is ready. Of course, the administrator can also add or delete users manually, too.

  1. Connect to the Goodcrypto Server website.
  2. Sign in with the credentials you supplied when you configured your GoodCrypto Server.
  3. Click on the Mail menu item.
  5. Click on Users
  6. Click on Add user
  7. Fill in all the information, including the email address field for the user
    If you'd like to follow the convention that GoodCrypto uses, then the username and email address will be the same.
  8. Don't forget to sign out when you're finished.

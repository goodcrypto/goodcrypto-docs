How do I know if a message I send will be encrypted?

Check if you have an encryption key for your contact.

  1. Browse to your GoodCrypto private server.
  2. Click "Mail"
  3. Under "Keys" click "View ID"
  4. Enter your contact's email address.
If there's a fingerprint for the email's key, your message will be encrypted.

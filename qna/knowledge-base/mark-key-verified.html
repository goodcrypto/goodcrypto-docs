How do I mark a key as verified?

You must be logged in to mark a key as verified.

  1. Connect to the Goodcrypto Server website.
  2. Sign in
  3. Click on Mail in the menu
  4. Click on Fingerprint
  5. Enter the Email address and select the type of encryption
  6. Add a check mark to Verified
  7. Click OK

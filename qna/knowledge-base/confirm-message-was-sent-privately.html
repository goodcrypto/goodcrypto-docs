How do I confirm a message was sent privately?

Only the sender of a message may confirm if a message was sent privately.

  1. Connect to your Goodcrypto Server website
  2. Sign in with the credentials for the user that sent the message
  3. Click the Mail menu
  4. Click the Sent
  5. Scan for the recipient's email address, date, and message-id from the header of the message

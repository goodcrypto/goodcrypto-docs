Why should I pay for open source?

  1. You get the fastest security updates possible.
  2. You get the newest features first, typically 6 months or more before non-subscribers
  3. You're not vulnerable while you wait for the next source release.
  4. You don't have to build and integrate updates manually.
  5. You help finance continued development.
  6. You get professional support. No finger pointing. Just solutions.
